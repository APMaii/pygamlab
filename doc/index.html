PyGamLab V2

PyGamLab is a scientific Python library developed for researchers, engineers, and students in materials science and nanotechnology. It provides tools for building and visualizing atomic structures, connecting to material databases, loading and fine-tuning pre-trained AI models, analyzing experimental data, and using fundamental constants, conversion utilities, and engineering formulas.

PyGam Research Group

PyGamLab V2 is developed by the PyGam Research Group, a team of researchers and engineers dedicated to advancing materials science through computational tools and open-source software. Our mission is to provide researchers worldwide with powerful, accessible tools for materials discovery and analysis.

Our Vision

To democratize access to advanced computational materials science tools and accelerate scientific discovery through collaborative development and open-source innovation.

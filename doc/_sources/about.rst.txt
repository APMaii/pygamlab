About PyGamLab
==============

**PyGamLab** stands for *Python GAMLAb tools*, a scientific Python library for nanostructure generation, alloy design, materials data exploration, and AI-driven analysis.

Developed at **GAMLab (Graphene and Advanced Material Laboratory)** under the supervision of **Prof. Malek Naderi** at **Amirkabir University of Technology (AUT)**.

**Key Contributors:**

- **Main Author:** Ali Pilehvar Meibody
- **Supervisor:** Prof. Malek Naderi
- **Co-Author:** Danial Nekoonam
- **Contributor:** Shokoofeh Karimi

**Features:**

- Nanostructure generation (0D, 1D, 2D, bulk)
- AI-driven property prediction
- Advanced data analysis and visualization
- Access to multiple materials databases

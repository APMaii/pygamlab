Functions Module
================

Provides a wide collection of **scientific formulas and utility functions** commonly used in engineering applications.

Examples:

- Thermodynamics equations (Gibbs free energy)
- Mechanical stress and strain calculations
- Fluid dynamics formulas
- General utility functions
